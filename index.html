QCOMICAL School 2027

on Quantum Programming Languages and their Semantics

July 19 to 23, 2027 - Colonia del Sacramento, Uruguay

Early preliminary announcement

After the first edition in Nancy (France) and the second in Pisa (Italy), the third QCOMICAL School will take place in Colonia del Sacramento (Uruguay) from July 19th to July 23rd, 2027, as part of the QCOMICAL project.

The school will feature tutorials and lectures by a diverse group of leading researchers, bringing together project members and external experts. Our program maintains a core focus on quantum programming languages and their semantics, fostering productive interaction across various foundational approaches in the field.
